### Gap 2: **India-Specific Tech Workforce Collapse (With Data)**
- **What it is:** Entry-level IT in India is getting hit harder than senior roles. Why? Execution-based careers are most exposed to automation.
- **Who's missing it:** The broader India tech creator space talks about "learning AI" but ignores the structural problem — their entire career pipeline was built on being cheaper at execution.
- **How Ayush owns it:** You're Indian, you understand the cultural context (relatives saying "get a TCS job, it's safe"). You can tell this story with empathy + data.
- **Angles:** "India's IT workforce isn't losing jobs because AI is smarter. It's losing jobs because they were trained to be cheap execution. Now something cheaper exists."

### Gap 3: **Production Reality vs. CEO Hype**
- **What it is:** Mustafa Suleyman says 18 months to full white-collar automation. Reality: all-LLM systems cost $3,270/month and fail 20% of the time. Hybrid systems cost $860 and work.
- **Who's missing it:** Tech discourse is split between "PANIC AI IS COMING" and "AI won't change anything." Nobody's doing the nuanced "here's what actually works in production."
- **How Ayush owns it:** You have production MLOps experience. You can call bullshit on the demo vs. deployment gap with real numbers.
- **Angles:** "The demo always works. Production is different." Use the $3,270 vs $860/month data to show that companies going all-in on automation are quietly rebuilding with humans in the loop.

### Gap 4: **The Sorting Event (Judgment vs. Execution)**
- **What it is:** The market isn't replacing everyone. It's sorting: people paid for judgment stay. People paid for execution get compressed.
- **Who's missing it:** Everyone's either catastrophizing ("all jobs gone") or reassuring ("nothing changes"). Nobody's doing the honest middle ground.
- **How Ayush owns it:** You've lived this — SBL, ZenML before MLOps, rebuilding from scratch. You can frame this as "here's which side you're on and what to do about it."
- **Angles:** "Will AI take your job? Depends if you're being paid for judgment or execution."

### Gap 5: **Vulnerability + Rebuilding (Personal)**
- **What it is:** The real power move is going through a breakdown and coming out the other side with real depth.
- **Who's missing it:** Everyone's posting "advice" and "frameworks." Nobody's posting "I was broken and here's what I built instead."
- **How Ayush owns it:** Your 2025 → 2026 arc is unique. Not "I didn't want to be alive" (too dark for public), but "I was quiet on social media rebuilding something real while everyone else was posting panic."
- **Angles:** "In 2025, I posted almost nothing. I was building. My feed was full of 'AI will replace you.' I was too busy to reply."
