Tweet 2:
The most common scam:

A tool that's $29/month offers "50% off" for Black Friday.

The catch? They're switching you to annual billing.

$29/month = $348/year
"50% off" annual = $174/year

That's not a deal. That's annual pricing with a countdown timer.

---

Tweet 3:
How to spot a REAL Black Friday AI deal:

✅ Discount applies to monthly billing too
✅ Lifetime deal — one payment, no recurring
✅ Extra credits or seats on top of the base plan
✅ Price not available any other time of year

If none of these apply — skip it.

---

Tweet 4:
The 3 tiers worth actually buying:

🟢 $0 — Extended free trials (30–90 days). No card needed. Take them all.

🟡 $29–49 — Lifetime deals on tools you've already tested. Don't buy blind.

🔴 $99+ — Annual plans on tools you already pay monthly. Run the math first.
