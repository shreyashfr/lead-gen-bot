## Research Findings

**The AI Washing Phenomenon**
- Sam Altman just admitted (Feb 19-20) companies falsely blame AI for restructuring layoffs
- This reframes narrative from "AI replacing jobs" to "companies using AI as PR cover"
- Massive signal: the scapegoat narrative is cracking

**The Data Paradox**
- 50K+ layoffs cited AI in 2025 (Challenger Gray)
- 37,478 people laid off in 2026 so far = 721/day
- BLS data: NO statistically significant change in unemployment for high-AI-exposure jobs post-ChatGPT
- Fear ≠ reality gap is the actual story

**Microsoft AI CEO Narrative**
- Mustafa Suleyman: all white-collar work automated in 18 months
- Viral claim this week, driving macro fear
- Counter with production reality

**India Specificity**
- Wave of suicides among India's IT workforce (Futurism)
- Entry-level jobs hit hardest
- Mid/senior largely intact (The Register)
- This is Ayush's insider angle

**The Counternarrative**
- IBM tripling entry-level hiring (contradicts panic)
- Mark Cuban: AI is "hungover college intern" with $100K price tag
- Real automation hasn't materialized at scale
