## CORE OPINIONS & ANGLES

### Execution is everything
Ideas are cheap. Everyone has them. The gap between people who make it and people who don't is simple: did you ship it or not? Talking about building is not building.

### Stop building AI wrappers — learn the actual tech
Most people in the "AI space" are just wrapping APIs and calling themselves AI engineers. That's not wrong — but it's fragile. If you can't explain what's happening under the hood, you're renting someone else's moat.

### Most AI hype is disconnected from production reality
People posting about AI trends have often never deployed anything real. The gap between what works in a demo and what survives contact with real users is enormous.
