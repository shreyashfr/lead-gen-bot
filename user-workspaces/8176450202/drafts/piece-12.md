## SLIDE 2: THE COST PROBLEM 💰

**Why India Can't Bet on Giant Models**

ChatGPT-scale models need massive GPUs.  
Inference costs: ₹1000s per day.

Smaller models (SLMs)?  
Run locally. Cost ₹100s per month.  
No cloud dependency.

For a country with 500M+ students — this is the only way forward.

---

## SLIDE 3: RURAL TECH OPPORTUNITY 🌾

**The Real Game-Changer**

80% of India lives outside big cities.  
Internet? Spotty. Expensive.

Small models run offline.  
No constant internet needed.  
No latency spikes.

Education, healthcare, banking — suddenly possible in villages.

Your code can reach places big models can't.
