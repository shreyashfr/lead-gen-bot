We shipped 7 features in 60 days.

Here's what went out the door.

1. Post Engagement Leads. Turns LinkedIn post commenters and likers into warm outreach targets. If they're already engaging with your content, they're not cold.

2. Flexible Outreach Hours. You pick exactly when messages go out. Respects time zones, doesn't make the account look like it never sleeps.

3. Fractional SDRs. Real human profiles handle outreach instead of your own account. Your pipeline grows. Your account stays clean.

4. Attachment Support. Send decks, case studies, or one-pagers directly in the sequence. Context at the right moment beats a follow-up ask.

5. ICP Filter. Tighten who gets contacted based on your exact customer profile. Better targeting, less noise.

6. Smart Followups. The system reads where each conversation is and sends the right next message. No more one-size-fits-all sequences dragging down reply rates.

7. Curated Leads. Pre-vetted contacts in your niche, ready to go. Less time building lists, more time on conversations that matter.

60 days. 7 features.

We're not done.
