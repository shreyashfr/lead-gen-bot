Vibe Outreach levels. The faster you hit level 5, the more pipeline you build.

Level 0
You write every cold email by hand. Open a blank doc, spend 20 minutes on something that starts with "I hope this finds you well." Send 8-10 a day. Wait.

Level 1
You grab a template. Swap the name and company. Add a Gmail extension for follow-ups. It looks like outreach. The reply rate tells a different story.

Level 2
You set up a proper sequencer. Apollo, Lemlist, Instantly. CSV upload, 3-touch sequence, automated follow-ups. You've removed the manual work. But you're still writing every message yourself and wondering why it's not converting.

Level 3
You add AI to write the messages. Paste the prospect's LinkedIn into GPT, get a draft, clean it up, load it into the sequence. Writing time drops 70%. You're still the bottleneck, just a faster one.

Level 4
You build the full stack. Clay for enrichment, GPT for copy, a sequencer for delivery. It works. It also took 3 weeks to set up, breaks when APIs change, and now you're basically a part-time data engineer.

Level 5
You write a prompt. Who to reach, what angle to use, what you want them to do. Sbl.so handles the sourcing, the messaging, the sequences, the follow-ups. You look at replies.

Most people are at Level 2 and calling it Level 4.
